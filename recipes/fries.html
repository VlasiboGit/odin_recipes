French Fries

Easy French Fries recipe

Who doesn't like french fries?

Ingredients

  • Frying oil
  • 1 kg potatoes

Method

  1. peel potatoes and slice them 1.5 cm thick
  2. heat oil to ca 200 degrees celsius
  3. rinse these potato sticks for a couple of times in water
  4. place them on a clean kitchen cotton napkin.
  5. fry until golden brown
  6. enjoy
